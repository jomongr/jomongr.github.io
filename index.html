01
Robotics ROS2 · SLAM
Autonomous Inventory Robot with SLAM Navigation
A fully autonomous mobile robot designed for warehouse inventory management — simulated end-to-end in ROS and Gazebo. The robot localizes on a pre-built map, plans collision-free paths, and navigates dynamically to target locations without human input.
  • Modeled the complete robot in URDF with continuous wheel joints, LiDAR sensor, and RGB camera including inertial and collision properties
  • Implemented AMCL localization fusing LiDAR scan data against a pre-built occupancy map for real-time pose estimation
  • Configured the move_base navigation stack with DWA local planner and custom global/local costmaps for obstacle-aware path planning
  • Integrated wheel odometry feedback and velocity command publishing over cmd_vel for precise motion control

Hardware · MATLAB
Dual-Axis Solar Tracker — Simulink Control Model
A closed-loop control system for a dual-axis solar tracking mechanism, modeled and simulated in MATLAB Simulink. The system continuously adjusts panel orientation in both azimuth and elevation axes based on sensor feedback to maximize solar energy capture throughout the day.
  • Designed PID control loops for both horizontal and vertical axes with sensor-based feedback for real-time orientation correction
  • Simulated sun-tracking performance across a full day cycle, validating energy yield improvement over fixed-panel baseline
  • Modeled actuator dynamics and sensor noise in Simulink for realistic closed-loop system evaluation

Safety Click Razor — Sustainable Redesign via Additive Manufacturing
A mechanical redesign of a consumer safety razor with a focus on sustainability and waste reduction.
